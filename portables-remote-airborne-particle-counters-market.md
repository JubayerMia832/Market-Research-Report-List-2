<p><p>The portable remote airborne particle counters market is divided into two main types: ionizing and non-ionizing. Ionizing particle counters utilize charged particles to detect and measure airborne contaminants, typically providing high sensitivity and accuracy. Non-ionizing counters, on the other hand, employ light scattering or other non-invasive techniques to measure particle concentration without altering the particles. Both types serve critical roles in monitoring air quality in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, cleanrooms, and environmental research, ensuring compliance with safety standards.</p></p>

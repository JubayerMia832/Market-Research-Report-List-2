<p><strong>The GaAs Solar Cell Epitaxial Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three Junction</li><li>Double Junction</li><li>Single Junction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaAs solar cell epitaxial wafer market is segmented into three types: single junction, double junction, and three junction cells. Single junction cells consist of a single absorption layer, offering moderate efficiency. Double junction cells combine two layers to enhance efficiency and better capture sunlight. Three junction cells, composed of three layers, maximize energy conversion by utilizing a broader spectrum of light. Each type serves different applications, with three junctions being ideal for concentrated photovoltaics and space applications, while single junctions are more cost-effective for everyday use.</p></p>

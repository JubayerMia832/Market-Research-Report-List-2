Wood beds are popular for their natural look and ease of customization, though they may require maintenance against rot. Metal beds offer durability and modern aesthetics, often resistant to pests but can heat up quickly in the sun. Polypropylene resin beds are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to weathering, providing a low-maintenance, long-lasting option. Each type caters to different gardening preferences and environments.</p></p>

<p><p>The rotary tiller market is primarily divided into two types: front-tine and rear-tine tillers. Front-tine tillers have tines located at the front, making them ideal for smaller gardens and lighter soil, as they are more maneuverable. Rear-tine tillers, on the other hand, have tines positioned at the back, providing greater power and stability for larger areas and tougher soil conditions. Each type caters to different user needs, from amateur gardeners to professional landscapers, influencing their popularity in the market.</p></p>

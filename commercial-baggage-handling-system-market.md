<p><p>The Commercial Baggage Handling System market is primarily divided into two types: RFID and Barcode systems. RFID Commercial Baggage Handling Systems leverage radio frequency identification technology to automate tracking and manage baggage flow efficiently, offering enhanced accuracy and real-time monitoring. In contrast, Barcode Commercial Baggage Handling Systems utilize printed barcodes scanned at various checkpoints to ensure proper handling and tracking of luggage. Both systems aim to improve operational efficiency, reduce mishandling, and enhance the overall passenger experience at airports.</p></p>

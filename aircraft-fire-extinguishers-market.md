<p><p>Aircraft fire extinguishers are categorized into two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed extinguishers are permanently installed within the aircraft, designed to activate automatically or manually in case of a fire, offering continuous protection. Portable extinguishers, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be easily moved and deployed by crew members as needed. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in aviation, providing effective suppression of fires and minimizing damage during emergencies.</p></p>

<p><p>The magnetic therapy instrument market features primarily two types: Magnetostatic Therapy and Dynamic Magnetic Therapy. Magnetostatic Therapy utilizes static magnetic fields to promote healing and alleviate pain by enhancing blood circulation and cellular function. In contrast, Dynamic Magnetic Therapy employs changing magnetic fields to stimulate tissue and improve biological responses. Both therapy types are used to manage various conditions, including chronic pain, inflammation, and injuries, offering non-invasive alternatives to traditional treatments while attracting interest in holistic health practices.</p></p>

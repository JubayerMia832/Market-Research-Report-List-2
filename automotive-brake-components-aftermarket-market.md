<p><p>The automotive brake components aftermarket includes essential parts that enhance vehicle braking systems. Brake pads provide friction against rotors, while brake rotors serve as the surface for the pads. Brake shoes are used in drum brake systems, paired with brake drums that encapsulate them. Brake calipers clamp the pads onto the rotors to create stopping power, and brake hoses transmit brake fluid under pressure. Each component plays a crucial role in ensuring safety and performance in vehicles.</p></p>

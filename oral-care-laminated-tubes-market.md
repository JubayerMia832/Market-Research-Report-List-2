<p><strong>The Oral Care Laminated Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oral Care Laminated Tubes market comprises two primary types: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminates) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminates). ABL tubes feature a combination of aluminum and plastic layers, providing excellent barrier properties against moisture and light, ideal for toothpaste and other oral care products. PBL tubes, made predominantly from plastic materials, offer flexibility and are lightweight, making them suitable for a variety of applications, though they may provide less protection compared to ABL variants. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and product needs.</p></p>

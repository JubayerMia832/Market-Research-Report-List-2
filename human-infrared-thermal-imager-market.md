<p><strong>The Human Infrared Thermal Imager Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photon Detection</li><li>Thermal Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Human Infrared Thermal Imager market comprises two primary types: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection relies on sensors that respond to infrared radiation emitted by objects, allowing for high-resolution imaging in low-light conditions. In contrast, thermal detection measures the temperature variations of surfaces, capturing an image based on emitted heat rather than light. Both technologies are crucial in applications like surveillance, medical diagnostics, and industrial monitoring, enhancing the ability to visualize thermal profiles and identify anomalies in various environments.</p></p>

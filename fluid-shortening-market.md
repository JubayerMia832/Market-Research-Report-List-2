<p><ul><li>Normal Type</li><li>Stable Type</li><li>High Emulsification Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluid shortening markets are categorized into three types based on their emulsification properties. Normal type fluid shortenings are versatile and commonly used in baking, providing moderate emulsification. Stable type fluid shortenings offer stronger emulsification, ideal for products needing greater structure and stability, such as spreads. High emulsification type fluid shortenings excel in creating emulsions, making them suitable for applications like creams and fillings where a silky texture is essential. Each type fulfills specific needs in the food industry, catering to diverse product formulations.</p></p>

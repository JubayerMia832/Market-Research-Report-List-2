<p><strong>The Asphalt Surfactant Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cationic Additive</li><li>Anion Additive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt surfactant additives market is categorized mainly into cationic and anionic additives. Cationic additives are positively charged and enhance adhesion between asphalt and aggregates, improving the durability of asphalt mixtures. They are often used in applications requiring water resistance. Anionic additives, on the other hand, carry a negative charge and are effective in reducing surface tension and improving the workability of asphalt. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and longevity of asphalt products in construction and road paving.</p></p>

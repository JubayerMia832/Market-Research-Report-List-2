<p><strong>The HVAC Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the HVAC pumps market, two primary types are single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed to deliver fluid at a single pressure level, making them suitable for systems requiring low to moderate pressure. They are typically more compact and energy-efficient. Conversely, multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers, enabling them to generate higher pressures, ideal for applications needing significant elevation or long-distance fluid transfer. This versatility makes them essential for larger or more complex HVAC systems.</p></p>

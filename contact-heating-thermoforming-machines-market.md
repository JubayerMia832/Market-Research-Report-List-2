Semi-Automatic machines assist in the process, offering a balance between human intervention and automation for improved productivity. Automatic thermoforming machines are fully automated, minimizing human involvement and maximizing speed and consistency in production. Each type serves different scales of operation and production needs within the thermoforming industry.</p></p>

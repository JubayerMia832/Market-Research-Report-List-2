<p><ul><li>Roof Mounting</li><li>Ground Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV racking and mounting equipment market comprises two primary types: roof mounting and ground mounting. Roof mounting involves installing solar panels directly on building rooftops, leveraging existing structures and minimizing land use. This method is often more cost-effective and suitable for residential and commercial applications. Ground mounting, on the other hand, involves installing solar panels on the ground in open spaces, allowing for larger systems and optimal positioning for sunlight exposure, making it ideal for utility-scale projects.</p></p>

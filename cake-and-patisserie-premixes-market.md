<p><p>The cake and patisserie premixes market consists of three main types: complete mixes, dough-base mixes, and dough concentrates. Complete mixes provide all necessary ingredients, simplifying preparation for end-users. Dough-base mixes contain pre-prepared dough, requiring minimal additional components, ideal for quick baking. Dough concentrates are semi-finished products that require more blending and handling, allowing for customization while reducing labor time. Each type caters to different baking needs, enhancing efficiency and consistency in producing cakes and pastries.</p></p>

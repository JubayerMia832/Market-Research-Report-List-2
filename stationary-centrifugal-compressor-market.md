<p><ul><li>Single Stage Centrifugal Compressors</li><li>Multi Stage Centrifugal Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary centrifugal compressor market is divided into two main types: single stage and multi-stage centrifugal compressors. Single stage centrifugal compressors consist of one impeller and are designed for applications requiring moderate pressure increases, making them efficient and cost-effective for lower pressure needs. In contrast, multi-stage centrifugal compressors utilize multiple impellers to achieve higher pressure ratios, suitable for demanding applications like gas transportation and industrial processes where efficiency and capacity are critical in compact designs.</p></p>

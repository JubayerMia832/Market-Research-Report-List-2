<p><p>The automatic refractometer market is divided into two main types: desktop and handheld refractometers. Desktop automatic refractometers are larger, often used in laboratories and industrial settings for precise and high-throughput measurements of refractive index. They provide advanced features and high precision, suitable for complex samples. In contrast, handheld automatic refractometers are portable, designed for field use, and cater to quick and easy measurements. These devices are favored in applications like agriculture and food processing where mobility and convenience are essential.</p></p>

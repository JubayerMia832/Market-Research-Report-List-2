<p><p>The immunodiagnostic reagent market comprises various types, including electrochemiluminescence, colloidal gold, chemiluminescence, and isotope-based methods. Electrochemiluminescence utilizes light generated through chemical reactions for sensitive detection, while colloidal gold employs gold nanoparticles for rapid visual results in tests like lateral flow assays. Chemiluminescence involves light emission from chemical reactions, providing high sensitivity. Isotope-based methods use radiolabeled substances, enabling specific detection and quantification of targets. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to varied diagnostic needs and research applications.</p></p>

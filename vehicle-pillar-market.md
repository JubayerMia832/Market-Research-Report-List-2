B Pillar serves as the support between front and rear doors, crucial for passenger safety. C Pillar is located at the rear of the vehicle, contributing to design and structural support, while D Pillar often refers to the area surrounding the rear windshield in larger vehicles. Each pillar plays a role in safety, design, and performance.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Soda-lime</li><li>Synthetic Quartz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TFT-LCD photomask market comprises two primary types: soda-lime and synthetic quartz. Soda-lime photomasks are cost-effective and commonly used for applications requiring less precision, making them suitable for standard LCD manufacturing. In contrast, synthetic quartz photomasks offer superior optical properties and thermal stability, making them ideal for high-resolution and advanced display technologies. These materials are essential in producing precise patterns on substrates, ultimately influencing the efficiency and quality of TFT-LCD panel production.</p></p>

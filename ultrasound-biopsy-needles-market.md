<p><p>The ultrasound biopsy needles market comprises several types, including Endoscopic Ultrasound Fine Needle Aspiration (EUS-FNA), Endoscopic Ultrasound Fine Needle Biopsy (EUS-FNB), and others like Endobronchial Ultrasound (EBUS).

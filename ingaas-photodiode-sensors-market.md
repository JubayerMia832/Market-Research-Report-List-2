<p><p>The InGaAs photodiode sensor market features two primary types: multi-element arrays and single-element arrays. Multi-element arrays are further categorized into PN, PIN, and avalanche structures, which enhance performance by allowing efficient light detection and improved sensitivity across various wavelengths. Multi-element arrays are ideal for applications requiring high-speed and precision measurements. In contrast, single-element arrays offer simplicity and are used in applications where lower complexity and cost are beneficial. Both types play essential roles in telecommunications, spectroscopy, and sensing technologies.</p></p>

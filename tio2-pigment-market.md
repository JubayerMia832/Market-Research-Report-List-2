<p><strong>The TiO2 Pigment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TiO2 pigment market is primarily divided into two production processes: the sulfate process and the chloride process. The sulfate process involves the reaction of titanium ore with sulfuric acid, resulting in a high-quality pigment suitable for a variety of applications, especially in coatings and plastics. In contrast, the chloride process uses titanium tetrachloride, offering superior purity and brightness, making it ideal for applications like high-performance coatings. Each method influences pigment characteristics, pricing, and market demand significantly.</p></p>

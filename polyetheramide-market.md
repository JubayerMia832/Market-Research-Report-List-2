<p><p>The polyetheramide market is segmented into three main types: monoamines, diamines, and triamines. Monoamines consist of single amine groups, serving applications that require mild reactivity and flexibility. Diamines, featuring two amine groups, provide enhanced curing properties and are often used in high-performance coatings and adhesives. Triamines, with three amine groups, offer superior cross-linking capabilities, making them ideal for demanding structural applications. Each type plays a distinct role in the production of robust, resilient materials across various industries.</p></p>

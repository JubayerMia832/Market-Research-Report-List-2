<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Keyless Go market consists of two primary types: Passive Keyless Access (PKES) and Remote Keyless Access (RKES). PKES enables drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physically pressing buttons, using proximity sensors that detect the key fob nearby. In contrast, RKES allows users to unlock and start the car remotely with a button press on the key fob. Both systems enhance convenience and security, catering to modern consumer preferences for seamless vehicle access and control.</p></p>

<p><p>The siphonic one-piece toilet market is characterized primarily by two types: jet siphon and vortex siphon. Jet siphon toilets utilize a powerful jet action to create a strong flushing force, effectively removing waste with minimal water usage. In contrast, vortex siphon models employ a swirling action that enhances the flushing performance by efficiently drawing water and waste into the bowl, promoting effective drainage. Both designs focus on efficiency, cleanliness, and water conservation, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers.</p></p>

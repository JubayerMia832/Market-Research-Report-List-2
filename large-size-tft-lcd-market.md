<p><p>The large-size TFT LCD market comprises various display technologies, each with distinct characteristics. TN (Twisted Nematic) panels offer fast response times but limited viewing angles. IPS (In-Plane Switching) panels provide superior color accuracy and wider viewing angles, making them popular for high-quality displays. AFFS (Advanced Fringe Field Switching) enhances IPS technology by improving brightness and viewing angles. MVA (Multi-Domain Vertical Alignment) panels excel in contrast and wider viewing angles, while other emerging technologies continue to expand market options and applications.</p></p>
